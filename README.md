# Project-1-Guidelines
Part A – Meetup Participation
Create a Meetup.com account
Join a Computer Science-related Meetup group
Attend one Meetup event (virtual or in-person)
Meet and talk with one professional attendee
Create your slide deck (Slides 1–6)
• Slide 1: Meetup group name and screenshot
• Slide 2: Event title, location, date, time
• Slide 3: Presenters and background
• Slide 4: What was discussed
• Slide 5: Who you met and their answer
• Slide 6: Your reflection
Part B – Getting Engaged in Technology
Attend a campus tech-related meeting (Slide 7)
Create a Stack Overflow account, find a related question, and add to Slide 8
Create a GitHub account, make a project board for this assignment, and add to Slide 9
Create a ChatGPT account, ask the same question from Stack Overflow, and compare results on Slide 10
Submit your slide deck and video presentation
